# Research Plan: Health Insurance Payer Outreach Strategy for Clinical Reasoning Engine Platform

## Task Overview
Research how to approach health insurance companies (payers) with a clinical reasoning engine platform and create a comprehensive outreach strategy.

## Research Components

### Phase 1: Market and Decision-Maker Research
- [x] 1.1 Identify key decision-makers at insurance companies (medical directors, innovation teams, etc.)
- [x] 1.2 Research organizational structures of major payers
- [x] 1.3 Understand decision-making processes and buying committees

### Phase 2: Value Proposition Development
- [x] 2.1 Research how clinical AI saves insurers money
  - [x] 2.1.1 Reduced hospitalizations and readmissions
  - [x] 2.1.2 Better care management outcomes
  - [x] 2.1.3 Early intervention and preventive care
  - [x] 2.1.4 Cost savings data and ROI metrics
- [x] 2.2 Document clinical outcomes and quality improvements
- [x] 2.3 Research regulatory and compliance benefits

### Phase 3: Case Studies and Partnerships
- [x] 3.1 Research AI healthcare startups that have successfully partnered with payers
- [x] 3.2 Document partnership models and structures
- [x] 3.3 Analyze success factors and common challenges
- [x] 3.4 Extract lessons learned and best practices

### Phase 4: Market Analysis
- [x] 4.1 List major insurers in Texas/DFW market
- [x] 4.2 List major national payers
- [x] 4.3 Research market size and growth trends
- [x] 4.4 Identify competitive landscape

### Phase 5: Outreach Strategy Development
- [x] 5.1 Develop multi-touch outreach campaign strategy
- [x] 5.2 Create messaging framework for different stakeholders
- [x] 5.3 Identify optimal contact channels and timing
- [x] 5.4 Develop pitch deck and supporting materials outline
- [x] 5.5 Create relationship building and partnership proposal structure

### Phase 6: Final Report Creation
- [x] 6.1 Synthesize all research findings
- [x] 6.2 Create comprehensive insurance_strategy.md document
- [x] 6.3 Include actionable recommendations and next steps

## Success Criteria
- Complete analysis of payer decision-making structure
- Documented value proposition with supporting data
- Minimum 3-5 case studies of successful AI-payer partnerships
- Comprehensive list of Texas/DFW and national payers
- Actionable outreach strategy with messaging framework
